# Coke Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive data analysis of Coca-Cola sales performance data. 

## Key Insights Included:
- **Executive Dashboard:** Total revenue of $8.68M across 17.14M units sold with a 36.3% operating margin.
- **Product Performance:** Coca-Cola is the primary revenue driver ($2.01M), followed closely by Dasani Water ($1.72M).
- **Regional & Retailer Analysis:** The West region is our top performing territory ($2.83M), and Sodapop is our largest retail partner ($4.40M).
- **Seasonality:** Sales experience a significant peak during the summer months, topping out in July ($1.04M).
